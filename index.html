Favorite Sports Teams

Manchester United
Arguably the biggest soccer team in the world and have been my favorite team since the 1999 classic FA Cup semi-final game against Arsenal.
Pittsburgh Penguins
My mom being from Pittsburgh and never having an NHL team in Cleveland, the Pens were the obvious choice for me. Also if i picked anyone else my Grandmother probably would have disowned me.
Cleveland Indians
Growing up in Cleveland my family has always been a huge baseball family. My Grandfather played pro-ball in the 40's before being drafted, my dad coached Berea High School to 10 state finals, winning 8 of them. Baseball is a family passion passed down from generation to generation along with our love of the Tribe.
Olympique Lyonnais
The thing that attracted me most to Lyon is their insane and die-hard fan base, their chants, their attitude, everything about the team appealed to me.
Columbus Crew
At the moment i have a very torrid love affair with the Crew. I've been watching and going to Crew games since a very young age, but now the crooked owner is trying to move the team to Austin, Texas and i have to decide if watching and supporting what maybe their last year of their existence is worth the heartbreaks i know will surely come at season's end.

About Me

I am 28 years old. I am a veteran retail manager and current barista at a 24 hour coffee shop in Cleveland. I am taking the Cleveland Codes program at Tri-C Metro to hopefully land a career in software development and with the guidance of our teacher and coding guru Sam Nasr i believe i will accomplish that goal.

In my spare time i love playing video games, watching movies, reading, and watching sports. Watching soccer, hockey, and baseball is my favorite thing to do with friends, whether we go to a game or watch it on tv, we always manage to have a ton of fun.
